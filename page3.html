- Izakaya Mochiron -

Come on down to chow down at one of the best spots to eat Japanese cousine. They serve one of most tasty Gyudon and Ramen bowls and Takoyaki. Not to mention their cool looking restaurant.

Into anime? So are they, as you walk in youl'll see at times they have cool action figures and art specially in their bathroom.

(Takoyaki)

My Favorites Menu:

  • Takoyaki- If you like seafood and specially octopus then this is definitely worth a try for first timers on trying Japanese food as an apetizer. Contains; Wheat flour-based batter and cooked in a special molded pan and chopped octopus in a ball-shaped form. - WARNING! It is extremely hot from the inside. Takoyaki is shown above picture.
    • -
  • Gyudon Bowl- Not into seafood? Very hungry? - No wormies, Gyudon bowls are a must have! And they serve a lot of rice and veggies and beef. As to why Gyudon really means (Beef Bowl). Contains; rice topped with beef and onion simmered in a mildly sweet sauce flavored with dashi and of your choice (soy sauce and mirin.)
    • -
  • Pork amen- As you have probably heard of this famous dish already. I absolutely love ramen bowls in this restaurant, they give a large quantity for ramen lovers. Contains; Onion, soy sauce, shichimi togarashi (Japanese seasoning), ginger, noodles, garlic, egg, and thin slice pork.
